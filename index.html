  • What crops can farmers list on the platform?

    Farmers can list a wide variety of crops including fruits, vegetables, grains, legumes, and more. The platform is designed to accommodate the diverse agricultural products grown by farmers.

  • How does the disease prediction feature work?

    The disease prediction feature leverages machine learning algorithms to analyze data on crop health, weather patterns, and other factors to provide early warnings of potential disease outbreaks. This allows farmers to take proactive measures to protect their crops.

  • How does the AI chatbot feature assist farmers?

    The AI chatbot is designed to provide farmers with on-demand support and guidance on a wide range of topics, from crop management and pest control to regulatory compliance and business planning. Farmers can get quick answers to their questions.

  • Do you offer delivery services for consumer orders?

    Yes, the platform provides convenient delivery options for consumers, with the ability to select their preferred delivery timeframe and location
